A one-day workshop on behavioral clones — AI systems that model human decision-making and social behavior. Three invited keynotes (Danica Dillion, Marcel Binz, Nori Jacoby) anchor the day, with contributed talks, lightning talks, and a poster session in between. We close with a panel discussion and a BBQ.

    Organising committee: Levin Brinkmann & Dirk Wulff (Max Planck Institute for Human Development).
